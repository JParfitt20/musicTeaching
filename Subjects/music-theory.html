Music Theory

Music theory is a very important part of learning to play an instrument. It starts with learning the note names and their values but progresses onto musch more.

With music theory you will learn about all the conventions of how music is written so everyone will understand.

You will look at the pattern behind pieces to see how they are constructed and even begin to write some of your own music.

To be really fluent in both reading and writing music, requires a lot of practice so music theory will be a part of every lesson.
