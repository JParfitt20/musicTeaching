About Me

Hi! My name's Joanna and I've been teaching music since 2016. I teach piano and woodwind (flute, clarinet and saxophone). I'm a fully trained teacher with QTS and I have several diplomas in music. I have three diplomas on the piano and one on the flute.

I live just outside Norwich in the Norfolk countryside. Most of my time is spent going into local schools to teach pupils but I also teach pupils of all ages at home.

To find out more about what I teach, visit the 'Subjects' page.
